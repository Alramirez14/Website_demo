Beavers take over on namesake Beaver Creek

How these rodent engineers shape waterways in the Coast Range

  • 2 days ago
  • 2

While out collecting chemical samples this past January, Alec finds a small beaver dam and pond built accross the channel of Beaver Creek near the streamflow gauges. These big critters build reservoirs that create excellent habitat for anadromous fish to rear in, and it helps to slow and spread flashy Coast Range rain throughout the ecocsystem, but how to we manage these structures when they interfere with our study sites?
